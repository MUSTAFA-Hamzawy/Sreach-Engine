coretta scott king, known first as the wife of the rev. dr. martin luther king jr., then as his widow, then as an avid proselytizer for his vision of racial peace and nonviolent social change, died monday at a hospital in mexico. she was 78.      the primary cause of death was   insufficient cardio-respiratory,   which simply means her heart and breathing stopped, said dr. carlos guerrero tejada, who certified her death. the underlying causes were cerebral vascular disease and ovarian cancer, according to the death certificate.
