meet demodex, the face mite, a microscopic arachnid that lives on human skin. the pore is its humble abode and the waxy sebum we secrete is its meal of choice.
 
it s hard to know for sure, but there are likely many thousands of mites living on our faces at any given time, as bbc reel s melissa hogenboom explores.
